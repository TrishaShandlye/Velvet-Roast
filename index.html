Our Story

Velvet Roast is a place for people who love slow mornings, quiet corners and coffee that feels like a treat, not a habit. I wanted to create a café that feels warm, elegant and a little bit dreamy.

Every cup here is made with Attention and Care. The idea is simple: When you sit with your drink at Velvet Roast, you should feel Calm, Special and a Little more In Love With Life.

“Coffee is not just a drink here, it’s a mood.”

The Velvet Experience

  • Luxury cafe vibe with soft lighting and rich colors
  • Signature coffee blends created for a smooth, velvet feel
  • Calm space for conversations, reading or working
  • Desserts and drinks that look as good as they taste
